Highlights over the years

Since our founding in 1996, Friends has raised over $8.5 million to: protect wildlife and habitat, preserve cultural resources, promote environmental education, improve recreational trails, enhance visitor experiences, and build environmental stewardship at Saguaro National Park.

Fighting invasive plants

Volunteers help keep the Park's trail network free of various invasive plants.

Tracking wildlife

Wildlife cameras help scientists gather important data on different species in the Park.
